About Grim Hand

Grim Hand: A Dark Fantasy Poker RPG Grim Hand is a high-stakes tabletop adventure game set in a desolate, unforgiving world where fate is dealt, and survival hinges on both your cunning and the cards in your hand. This grimdark RPG blends strategic character progression with a unique poker-driven combat system, challenging players to navigate treacherous lands, face monstrous threats, and forge their own destiny.


At its heart, Grim Hand revolves around a compelling loop of Exploration, Combat, and Progression: Forge Your Champion: Begin by crafting your unique character, choosing from diverse Races (Human, Dwarf, Elf, Orc, Goblin), specializing in a Class (Warrior, Ranger, Rogue, Mystic, Scholar), and aligning with a powerful Affinity (Blood, Bone, Shadow, Iron, Nature). These choices define your starting abilities and influence your journey. Explore the Desolation: Each turn, you'll draw an Exploration Card, revealing a new location, a perilous event, or a hidden opportunity. These cards guide your journey and may lead you directly into conflict.


The Challenge Ahead Grim Hand is a game of calculated risks, tactical choices, and the ever-present shadow of fate. Will you master the cards, overcome the darkness, and carve your legend into this desolate world, or will your hand be your undoing? Gameplay for 1-5 players, with games lasting 60-120 minutes.

How to Play

1

Setup

Each player receives their starting hand of cards and dice. Place the deck in the center of play.

2

Play Cards

On your turn, play cards from your hand to build your strategy and affect other players.

3

Roll Dice

Roll your dice to determine outcomes and activate special card abilities.

4

Win

Be the first player to achieve the victory condition and claim your Grim Hand!
